Pizza

Description

This recipe is to make a very basic cheese pizza that is easy and quick

Ingredients

  • Cheese
  • Tomato sauce
  • Pizza dough
  • Olive oil

Steps

  1. Rub olive oil on the pizza dough
  2. Add tomato sauce to the dough
  3. Sprinkle shredded cheese onto the pizza sauce
  4. Bake in the oven at 350 f for 15-20 minutes
